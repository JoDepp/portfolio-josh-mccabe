About Me

Hi, I'm Josh, teacher, artist and book author.

As a life long artists I have always been a creator. I enjoy many forms of artistic expression from drawing, painting, design, building and writting. My current passion is crafting childrens books. Inspired by my students, niece and nephews.


Professionally I am an innovative ESL educator with 12 years of teaching experience all over the world. I have a strong track record of improving my students learning opportunities through differentiation. I am excellent at developing relationships with students and building confidence in their abilities.
